ChatGPT: Creator or Terminator? How a Movie Pushed Me to Learn AI

Date: August 11, 2024

Every once in a while, you watch a movie that changes the way you think about the world—and for me, that movie was ChatGPT: Creator or Terminator?. As someone already curious about AI, this film felt like a wake-up call. It wasn’t just about the futuristic technology we see on the screen; it was about the real-life potential of AI and what we can do with it right now.

The movie explored the two opposing sides of AI: is it a tool to help humanity flourish, or could it become the very thing that puts us at risk? While the film leaned into some of the more dramatic, dystopian visions, it also highlighted the immense possibilities AI offers. It was this duality that struck me—AI can be as much a creator as it could be a destroyer, and how we use it is what matters most.

After watching, I knew I wanted to be on the side of AI as a creator. I started learning more about AI systems like ChatGPT and even enrolled in courses on AI Python coding to get a better grasp of the technology.
