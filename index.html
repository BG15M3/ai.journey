My Introduction to AI Prompt Engineering

Date: September 16, 2024

Have you ever wondered how to get more from AI, beyond just basic commands? I recently discovered that the secret lies in crafting better prompts!

I stumbled upon a TikTok by Sabrina Romanov, who explained how AI responds more effectively when you give it well-thought-out prompts. Already curious about AI, this immediately grabbed my attention. What really stood out, though, was when she mentioned the emerging role of Prompt Engineers—people who specialize in mastering the language of AI.

Sabrina recommended a course called "Beginners: Basics of AI Python Coding", so I decided to give it a try. It turned out to be a comprehensive introduction to AI that exceeded my expectations. The course taught me key terminology and how to interact with AI using Python and chatbots. One major takeaway was how even small adjustments to a prompt can significantly change the AI's response. This helped me see how powerful prompt engineering can be.

Now, I’m more excited than ever to continue my journey toward becoming a prompt engineer. I'll be sharing more about the tools I’m using and my ongoing progress, so stay tuned!
